Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Stackly services.

How does the Priority Applicant badge work?

When you apply for a job with Stackly Pro, your application is pushed to the top of the recruiter's ATS dashboard, increasing your visibility by up to 300%.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, all Stackly plans auto-renew but can be canceled at any time from your account settings without any hidden cancellation fees.

Is the AI Resume Builder ATS compliant?

Absolutely. Our resume builder uses strict single-column architecture and standard fonts guaranteed to be parsed correctly by Workday, Greenhouse, and Lever.

Are Government jobs verified?

Yes, all listings carrying the "Government" badge are authenticated through official state or federal employment API feeds.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all our Pro plans if you are not completely satisfied with the features provided.

How often are job listings updated?

Our platform synchronizes with corporate career portals and recruiter dashboards every hour to ensure you have access to the freshest opportunities.

Can I use Stackly Pro for multiple resumes?

Yes! You can generate and store up to 5 unique tailored resumes under the Pro plan to target different niches.

Is my personal data secure?

Your data privacy is our priority. We use end-to-end encryption and never sell your information to third-party data brokers.
